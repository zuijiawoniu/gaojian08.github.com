Brief Bio

I'm a Visiting Student in Macro Connections of the MIT Media Lab at Massachusetts Institute of Technology since Aug 2016, hosted by Prof Cesar A. Hidalgo. I'm also a PhD Student in CompleX Lab at University of Electronic Science and Technology of China since Sep 2014, under supervision of Prof Tao Zhou. Prior to that, I spent two years as a Master Student in School of Computer Science at UESTC. I received my Bachelor of Science degree from School of Mathematical Sciences at UESTC in 2012.

My research interests focus on understanding phenomena in social and economic systems through empirical and theoretical analysis. I'm working on solving a variety of problems that related to economic and networks, including regional industrial structure, economic complexity, spatial information spreading, vertex similarity of complex networks, etc. Please view profile on Google Scholar, Scopus,Research ID or directly download my CV.
