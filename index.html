Brief Bio

Jian Gao is currently a Ph.D. Student in CompleX Lab, Web Sciences Center, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). His research interests focus on understanding phenomena in social and economic systems through empirical and theoretical analysis. He is working with Prof. Tao Zhou and Dr Yanqing Hu (Sun Yat-sen University) in solving a variety of problems, including personalized recommendation in web-based systems, reputation evaluation in online rating systems, information spreading in spatial social networks, local industry structure modeling in China, etc. His work has been published in some peer-reviewed scientific journals, such as Nature Scientific Reports, Europhysics Letters, and Chinese Physics Letters.

Jian Gao received his BS in Information and Computing Science from UESTC in 2012. As undergraduate, he won the Outstanding Winner of the Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling (ICM) in 2012. He was awarded as the Outstanding Student of UESTC (the highest honour for undergraduate) in 2011. From 2012 to 2014, Jian Gao was a Master Student in School of Computer Science and Engineering, UESTC, majoring in Computational Intelligence. During that period, he worked with Prof. Tao Zhou, studying online ranking method and promotion/resignation predication in employee networks. In parallel, he served as a research assistant to Prof. Mingsheng Shang and Dr. Shimin Cai, studying recommender systems and missing value imputation. From 2012 to 2014, he also served as a Student Instructor in the Association of Mathematical Modeling of UESTC. He has found summer intern in two start-up companies, Hire Big Data, LTD. as an Algorithm Engineer in 2014 and Business Big Data, LTD. as a Data Analyst in 2015.

Now, I am looking for a group to be a Visiting Ph.D. Student or Visiting Scholar for one or two years. The visiting can be financially supported by China Scholarship Council (CSC). I really enjoy working on creative projects that enhance my technical skills and challenge me on a regular basis.
