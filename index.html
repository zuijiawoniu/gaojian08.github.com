Brief Bio

He received his Bachelor of Science degree (with highest honor) from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China, in 2012, majoring in Information and Computing Science. Prior to that, He graduated for Shangzhi (No. 1) High School, Harbin, China, in 2008. After two years’ master courses studying, he is currently a second-year Ph.D. Student in CompleX Lab, Web Sciences Center, UESTC.

Research Interests

  • Network Science: Network models, Percolation, Networked spreading processes

  • Recommender Systems: Recommender algorithms, Reputation based ranking methods

  • Data Analysis: Industry data analysis, Performance analysis and resignation predication
